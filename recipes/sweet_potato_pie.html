Sweet Potato Pie Recipe

Description

The filling consists of mashed sweet potatoes, evaporated milk, sugar, spices such as nutmeg, and eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmug
  • 1/2 teaspoon gound cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 9 inch unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Boil sweet potato whole in skin for 40 to 50 minutes, or until done. Run cold water over the sweet potato, and remove the skin.
  2. Break apart sweet potato in a bowl. Add butter, and mix well with mixer. Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla.
  3. Beat on medium speed until mixture is smooth. Pour filling into an unbaked pie crust.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 to 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Pie will puff up like a souffle, and then will sink down as it cools.
