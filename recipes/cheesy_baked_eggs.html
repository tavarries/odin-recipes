Cheesy Baked Eggs Recipe

Description

Baked eggs in a ramekin is a simple breakfast that can be ready in under 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Butter:For greasing the ramekin. Use salted or unsalted butter
  • Eggs:Two large eggs are all you need.
  • Cream:To keep this a low carb recipe, use heavy cream.
  • Cheese:I prefer using shredded cheddar cheese but you might like to consider using another type of cheese instead.
  • Parmesan cheese,salt,and black pepper, for wonderful flavor!

Steps

  1. Butter a small ramekin and set it aside while you whisk together 2 eggs, cream, shredded Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into the buttered ramekin and bake it in a preheated oven for 15-18 minutes.
